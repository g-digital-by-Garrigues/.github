## About g-digital

g-digital is Garrigues' digital business division, dedicated to driving innovation and technological development to assist our clients in their digital transformation.

Our mission is to develop technology solutions that apply our deep knowledge of the law to client processes, enhancing efficiency and legal certainty while opening up new business opportunities in an increasingly digital marketplace.

Our products are designed side-by-side by Garrigues lawyers — through [Garrigues Digital](https://www.garrigues.com/en_GB/garrigues-digital), our techlaw and digital-economy experts — and g-digital technologists, ensuring an optimal synthesis of technology and the law in every solution.

## Powered by EADTrust

Following Garrigues' acquisition of a 51% stake in [EADTrust](https://www.eadtrust.eu), a qualified trust service provider (QTSP) under the EU eIDAS Regulation, g-digital products are delivered through a secure, certified and fully regulated environment.

This combination is unique in the market: techno-legal solutions endorsed by Garrigues experts, implemented within a regulated framework that meets the highest compliance standards — so clients can operate with complete confidence in the most critical scenarios.

## Why this GitHub organization?

This space is the home for the **open-source components of the g-digital product ecosystem**.

Our first focus is publishing **Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers** for the g-digital product suite, so that developers and AI agents can interact with regulated digital trust services in a standardized, secure way. In other words: we are bringing **eIDAS-regulated trust services into the agentic AI era**.

### Our objectives

- 🔌 **Bridge AI agents and regulated digital trust.** Publish and maintain MCP servers that let LLM-based agents consume g-digital / EADTrust services — electronic signatures, qualified time stamps, certified notification and delivery, document custody and digital evidence — within a fully regulated framework.
- 📚 **Lower the integration barrier.** Provide reference implementations, SDKs and examples so developers can embed eIDAS-compliant capabilities into their workflows with minimal friction.
- 🤝 **Build a community around legal-grade AI.** Engage with the LegalTech, AI and digital-trust communities to define best practices for combining autonomous agents with legal certainty and regulatory compliance.
- 🧭 **Promote transparency in regulated technology.** Open up reference clients, documentation and tooling that support auditability of how our regulated services are consumed.

### Inside the EAD Enterprise Suite

The Enterprise Suite covers the digital trust cycle for enterprise environments through three integrated services:

- **[eArchiving](https://www.eadtrust.eu/en/legaltech-solutions/enterprise-suite/earchiving/)** — eIDAS-compliant digital custody and certification of critical files (contracts, schedules, source code, data rooms) in the European sovereign cloud.
- **[Signature Manager](https://www.eadtrust.eu/en/legaltech-solutions/enterprise-suite/signature-manager/)** — Electronic signatures for simple or advanced processes, with enhanced legal certainty and full confidentiality.
- **[Notice Manager](https://www.eadtrust.eu/soluciones-legaltech/enterprise-suite/notice-manager/)** — Certified electronic notification with the same evidentiary value as a bureaufax.
